Ingredients:
 - 1 onion
 - 1 can whole peeled tomatoes
 - 1 tbsp sugar
 - 1 tbsp salt
 - 2 tbsp olive oil

Directions:
 1. Slow cook the onion with the olive oil in a pot at low heat
 2. Add can of tomatoes, leave out some of the juice
 3. Add sugar and salt
 4. Let it simmer for an hour
 5. Blend it
